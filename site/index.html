Under the hood

The DSP is the headline.

Two hand-rolled analysis chains — monophonic pitch and polyphonic chords — running on every hardware buffer. No audio frameworks, no packages, pinned by synthesized-audio tests.

~2.7 ms
Input latency
A Core Audio HAL IOProc delivers every hardware buffer directly — the same approach DAWs use, because installTap's ~100 ms bursts are unusable for a tuner.
±5 cents
Tuner accuracy
YIN implemented from de Cheveigné & Kawahara (JASA 2002) in ~150 lines of vDSP, with parabolic interpolation for sub-sample period accuracy.
144 chords
Heard live
An FFT chromagram — peak-picked, harmonically sieved, sqrt-compressed — cosine-matched against templates with Schmitt-trigger hysteresis.
Pitch — YIN 2048-sample window · runs per 128-frame buffer
HAL IOProc capture
Every hardware buffer, straight from the input device — 128 frames ≈ 2.7 ms at 48 kHz, no tap batching.
Difference function d(τ)
Autocorrelation-style lag search over the window, vectorized with vDSP.
Cumulative mean normalization
The YIN step that suppresses the octave errors plain autocorrelation makes.
Threshold + parabolic fit
First dip under 0.15 wins; parabolic interpolation refines τ to sub-sample accuracy — frequency within a few cents across the guitar's range.
Chords — Chromagram 4096-sample window · 11.7 Hz bins
Hann window + FFT
Magnitude spectrum over the guitar's range, ~70 Hz–2 kHz.
Peak picking, interpolated
Bins are coarser than a semitone below ~330 Hz, so peak frequencies are recovered by parabolic interpolation instead of trusting bin centers.
Harmonic sieve
Peaks at 3×, 5×, or 7× a real peak are overtones, not played notes — the fifth's 5th harmonic is the root's major 7th — and get crushed before they vote.
12-bin chromagram
Pitch-class energy, √-compressed so a softly fretted third survives next to doubled open strings; adaptive smoothing, fast on attack, slow in sustain.
Template match + hysteresis
Cosine similarity against 144 chord templates, with acquire/hold thresholds on both confidence and signal level so the readout locks instead of flickering.
